Why this prototype

Capital One published Intent-Based Engineering on its tech blog — "TurboTax for developers": specify the desired outcome, skip the setup. This prototype extends IBE into the agentic workflow: AI generates the spec, writes tests first, codes to them, a different model reviews to catch drift, and the engineer signs off on evidence — not vibes.

Player-Coach signal. Shipped in hours, not weeks.
Independent review. Different LLM catches what the coder can't self-detect.
Fail-safe, not bottleneck. Human gate gets test results, QA, and spec-alignment scores.
Full DevOps loop. CI, agentic QA, canary deploy, armed rollback.
